BHIS(Billabong High International School)

Hi!

Billabong High International School (BHIS) is a chain of schools run by the Kangaroo Kids Education Ltd (KKEL) of Mumbai, India. The school has 23 branches at 16 locations throughout India. The schools are located in Mumbai, Noida, Chennai, Hyderabad, Vadodara, Bhopal, Jabalpur, Indore, Rewa, Puducherry, Coimbatore, Pukkattupadi, Thiruvallur, Kanchipuram, Kelambakkam, and Kanpur. The school operates from pre-primary to 12th-grade level and provides the checkpoint, Cambridge IGCSE.[1][2]
